Chimichangas are a delicious Mexican dish that involves deep-frying a filled tortilla. Follow this recipe to make your own tasty chimichangas at home!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken or beef
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Oil for frying
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, diced tomatoes, lettuce

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked shredded chicken or beef, shredded cheese, chopped onions, salsa, ground cumin, and chili powder. Mix until well combined.
  2. Place one flour tortilla on a flat surface. Spoon about 1/4 of the filling mixture into the center of the tortilla.
  3. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up from the bottom to the top to form a burrito-like shape.
  4. Heat oil in a deep skillet or frying pan over medium heat. The oil should be about 1-2 inches deep.
  5. Carefully place the rolled tortillas into the hot oil, seam side down. Fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the chimichangas from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve the chimichangas hot, topped with your favorite toppings such as sour cream, guacamole, diced tomatoes, and lettuce.

Enjoy your homemade chimichangas!
