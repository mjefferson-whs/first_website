Fajitas are a flavorful and colorful dish that is easy to make at home. Follow this recipe to prepare delicious fajitas!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast or skirt steak, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, sliced into strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese, cilantro

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, ground cumin, chili powder, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Add the sliced chicken or steak, and toss to coat. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken or steak and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until fully cooked and slightly charred. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and onions. Cook for 4-5 minutes until they are tender and slightly charred.
  4. Return the cooked chicken or steak to the skillet and toss everything together to combine.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas in a microwave or on a skillet.
  6. Serve the fajita mixture on the warm tortillas, and top with your favorite toppings like sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese, and cilantro.

Enjoy your homemade fajitas!
