Burritos are a versatile and satisfying meal, perfect for lunch or dinner. Here's how to make a delicious burrito at home!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 lb cooked ground beef or shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, lettuce, jalapeños, hot sauce

Instructions:

  1. Warm the flour tortillas in a microwave or on a skillet to make them more pliable.
  2. In the center of each tortilla, spread a layer of cooked rice, followed by black beans, ground beef or shredded chicken, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, guacamole, and chopped onions.
  3. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up from the bottom to the top to form a burrito.
  4. If you prefer a warm burrito, place the rolled burritos on a skillet over medium heat for 2-3 minutes on each side until lightly browned and heated through.
  5. Serve the burritos hot, topped with optional toppings like diced tomatoes, lettuce, jalapeños, and hot sauce.

Enjoy your delicious homemade burritos!
